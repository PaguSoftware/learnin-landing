Refund Policy

Last updated: July 23, 2025

Thank you for subscribing to Learnin. We are committed to providing a valuable service to our users. Please read our refund policy carefully.

1. Subscription Fees

Our Pro Plan is billed on a monthly subscription basis. All payments are processed at the beginning of each billing cycle. Due to the nature of our digital service and the immediate access to premium features, **monthly subscription fees are non-refundable.**

2. Cancellation Policy

You can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. When you cancel, your subscription will remain active until the end of your current paid billing period. You will not be charged for any subsequent billing cycles after the cancellation.

No partial refunds or credits will be issued for the remaining days of your subscription period if you cancel mid-cycle.

3. Exceptions

We do not offer refunds except where required by law. In rare, specific circumstances, we may consider offering a refund at our sole discretion. Any requests for exceptions must be submitted to our support team for review.

4. How to Cancel

You can manage and cancel your subscription directly from your user account dashboard. If you encounter any issues, please do not hesitate to contact our support team.
